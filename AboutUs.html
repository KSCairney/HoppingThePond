About Us.

We are Emily and Kenny. We met in London back in 2012 and have since lived in China, Japan and the USA. We have travelled as much as we can within the countries we have lived as well as in Poland, Germany, The Czech Republic, Italy, Ireland, South Korea, Scotland, Indonesia, Belgium and Hong Kong. This is our blog about our past travels, the gritty bits, the glorious bits and all the in-between bits. Travel is our passion and together our home is a stepping off point for continuing adventures.
